Citations

If you receive a parking ticket, either schedule an appeal (by fax or in-person) or pay the ticket (by mail, in-person, or online) within eighteen (18) calendar days of the ticket issue date. Failure to do so will result in additional fees.

Tickets are billed first to the permit holder and then to the vehicle's registered owner if no permit is displayed. Permit holders are responsible for all tickets issued to their permit, regardless of vehicle ownership.

If you have unpaid ticket charges, you will not be able to purchase a parking permit. Students with unpaid ticket charges may be barred from registering for classes and from receiving their diplomas or official transcripts. Your vehicle may be towed and impounded until all unpaid ticket charges are paid in full. Financial and academic holds can be viewed on Goldmine.
