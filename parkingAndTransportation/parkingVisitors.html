Visitor Parking

Several parking options are available to UTEP visitors:

  • 1. UTEP Sun Bowl Parking Garage
  • 2. Academic Services Building
  • 3. Hilton Garden Inn
  • 4. Metered Parking

1. UTEP Sun Bowl Parking Garage

Located near the corner of Sun Bowl Drive and University Avenue. This location serves as conference parking as well as a pay lot. See the posted rates and police enforcement hours at this location. Subject to availability, this visitor lot accepts credit cards as well as cash.

2. Academic Services Building

Located at the corner of Schuster Avenue and Hawthorne Street.The visitor parking section is clearly marked and is located within the S-1 parking lot. See the posted rates and police enforcement hours at this location. Subject to availability, this visitor lot accepts credit cards as well as cash.

3. Hilton Garden Inn

Located near the corner of University Avenue and Oregon Street. Parking at the Hilton Garden Hotel requires a Temporary Visitor Permit that you can obtain at the Guard Kiosk on University Avenue. Parking is subject to posted signs in the Hotel parking lot.

4. Metered Parking

Metered parking spaces are available throughout campus and are intended for short-term visitors. There is a one (1) hour time limit on parking in metered spaces. All those parking at metered parking spaces are required to pay the meter fee. There are no exceptions to this requirement. Visitor, faculty/staff, vendor, and student permits are not valid for parking in metered areas without paying the required meter fee.
