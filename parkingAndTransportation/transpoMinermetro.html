ADA Shuttle Times
  • Miner Metro shuttles are FREE to all UTEP faculty, staff, students and visitors.
  • The lost and found is operated by the Campus Police, please contact them at 747-6635 if you are looking for an item.
  • Service is available Monday through Friday when classes are in session during the fall, spring and summer semesters.
  • Shuttles do not operate during Wintermester, summer minimesters, University holidays or during intersessions.
