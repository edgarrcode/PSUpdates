Airline Reservations

Main Content

Business-related air travel must be arranged through the UT System Contracted Travel Agency. The following options are available for making airline reservations:

Toll Free: 1-866-366-1142
After Hour’s Emergency Service: 1-800-441-6512 (use V.I.T. Code S2P2A)

Either you or your department travel planner can reserve a flight

  1. The Agency will require the Travel Authorization Code and Funding Account Number
  2. Airfares will be paid by the University and departments will see the expenses charged back within the following month
  3. Concur

The following options are no longer available when booking your business-related air travel:

  1. Internet travel websites (i.e., Travelocity, Expedia, Orbitz, etc.)
  2. Direct airline purchase with the air carrier
  3. The University no longer supports the SWABIZ online booking tool

Exceptions for not using the UT System Contracted Travel Agencies:

  1. When travel is paid by an outside entity (not funded by the University)
  2. Extreme weather conditions
  3. Athletic Travel
  4. Study Abroad not funded by the University

Note: First class airfare may be purchased only if it was the only available. Business class airfare may be reimbursed only if a lower airfare was not available; a statement from a UT System Contracted Travel Agency is needed for supporting documentation.

If the state employee requires flying Business/First Class due to a medical necessity, the supporting documentation must state the first/business class airfare was medically necessary for the state employee. The supporting documentation for the expense/reimbursement must include the University’s ADA coordinator’s written documentation that the law or regulation requires the reimbursement.
