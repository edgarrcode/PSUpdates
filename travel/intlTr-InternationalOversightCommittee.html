International Oversight Committee

Main Content

In a memorandum from U.T. System dated April 22, 2010 institutional Presidents were directed to take certain precautionary measures with regard to University sanctioned international travel. These measures are necessary to ensure that the U. T. System minimizes risk and is in a position to assist faculty, staff or students on University sanctioned international travel in the event of an emergency.

President Natalicio has established UTEP’s International Oversight Committee responsible for assessing the risk of travel requested for countries where the U.S. Department of State has issued a current Travel Warning.

Role of the Oversight Committee

The role of the Oversight Committee is to review requests for exceptions to the International Travel Policy and make recommendations regarding the request to the Office of the President.
