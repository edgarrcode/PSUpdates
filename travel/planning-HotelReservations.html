Hotel Reservations

Main Content

State guidelines require travelers to use State Contracted hotels. The UT System Contracted Travel Agencies must be used when traveling internationally, including Canada, Mexico, and Puerto Rico.

Visit the Comptroller of Public Accounts website to see a hotel directory of "in state" and "out-of-state" contracted hotels.

Launch Comptroller of Public Accounts website

When reserving hotel accomodations a personal credit card will be required. The traveler is responsible for paying hotel expenses and paid receipts must be submitted to the department for reimbursement.

Exceptions for not using state contracted hotels:

  1. No state contracted hotel available for that city
  2. No available rooms at state contracted hotels
  3. Staying at hotel where seminar/conference is being held
  4. If lodging exceeds per diem rates local funds can be used for overage reimbursement

Note: A Texas State Tax Exemption Certificate must be presented at all Texas hotels. Travelers will not be reimbursed for Texas state occupancy taxes.
