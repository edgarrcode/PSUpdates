Travel Authorizations

Contact your department's travel planner, who is usually the administrative assistant, to submit a request for travel authorization. The following information is required:

  1. Department Head Approval
  2. Name of Traveler
  3. Destination
  4. Dates of Travel
  5. Purpose of Travel
  6. Estimated Costs and Account Information

Please Note: Appropriate arrangements for disposition of duties must be made in advance of travel if classes are to be missed, a qualified member of the instructional staff shall be identified to be approved by the department chair as a substitute instructor.

Travel Advances

A travel advance may be requested by contacting your department travel planner. Requests must be submitted to the Accounts Payable Department no earlier than 3 days no later than 10 days prior to travel. The state of Texas allows a travel advance for up to 90% of the per diem for meals and lodging. State approved meal and lodging per diems for your travel can be accessed using the the U.S. General Services Administration website..

All travel advance requests must have an "Approved" travel authorization and a signed "Authorization to Deduct Travel Advance from Payroll Checks" form. Unused travel funds must be turned into the University as soon as possible, and deposited at the Student Business Services Department to clear the advance.

Seminar/Conference Fees

The following options are available for paying your registration for seminars or conferences:

  • Registration is paid using the Pro-Card.
  • Registration is pre-paid by the University.
  • Submit your registration form to the department travel planner (i.e., administrative assistant) for pre-payment
  • Registration paid by employee and reimbursed by the University:
  • Submit your paid registration receipt to the department travel planner for reimbursement upon completion of travel.
