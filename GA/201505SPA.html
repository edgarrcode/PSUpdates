Audience Overview

The Strategic Planning and Assessment website had 24 unique users during the month of May.
These users generated 41 sessions (51.21% of these sessions were created by new visitors), resulting in 59 page views.
"Avg. Session Duration" is measured in seconds.

Browsers Used

Chrome was the most used browser by users.

Device Category (Desktop Vs. Mobile)

100% of the traffic used a desktop computer (not a mobile device).

Networks Used
