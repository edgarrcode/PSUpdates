HOP Website
July 2015 Analytics Report

Audience Overview

The HOP website had 651 unique users during the month of July.
These users generated 867 sessions (64.82% of these sessions were created by new visitors), resulting in 2,882 page views.
"Avg. Session Duration" is measured in seconds.

Browsers Used

Chrome was the most used browser by users.

Device Category (Desktop Vs. Mobile)

21% of the traffic used a tablet or a mobile device (not a desktop computer). 21% is a high number for mobile.

Networks Used

#1 represents that 425 (49.02%) of the sessions were by users in campus.

Acquisition Overview

Direct traffic: Users visited your site directly when opening the browser window (they don't come from any other website).
Organic Search: The traffic that comes from search engines.
Referral: Traffic that comes from other websites.
Social: Traffic generated by social media channels such as Facebook and Twitter.
