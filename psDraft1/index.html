PeopleSoft GoLive Countdown

Latest PeopleSoft News
January 13, 2014

The UTShare Executive Committee has determined that we will go live on May 5, 2014. The new date will give us additional time to finalize interim campus processes, communicate important changes to campus, and train our end users.

As the project moves forward, we will continue to communicate with our users on campus by email, e-newsletter, open forums, and the PeopleSoft website. Learn more about the change in go-live date by reading this memo from Dr. Scott C. Kelley, the Executive Vice Chancellor for Business Affairs at The University of Texas System.
