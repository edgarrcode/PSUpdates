UPK

One of the tools we plan to deploy early and make available to all campus users is something called the User Productivity Kit—or UPK. An Oracle product, UPK is a self-paced training tool that will allow you to learn specific tasks when and where it’s convenient for you.

To see examples of UPKs that have been developed at UTEP, check out the recently developed Miner Mall UPKs on the Miner Mall Training page. Just scroll down to the bottom of the page and click on any of the links in the table to start a UPK tutorial.

You can also try out these generic Oracle developed UPKs:

  • Employee Self Service
  • Time and Labor
  • Travel
  • Vouchers
  • Manage Funds
  • Pro-Card
