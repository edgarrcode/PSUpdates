    PeopleSoft Support

    If you have a PeopleSoft-related inquiry or issue, call the Help Desk at extension 4357 (off-campus: 747-5257) or email helpdesk@utep.edu.

    Learn More

  • Drop-In Help Sessions

    Beginning May 5, drop in the PeopleSoft Lab (Prospect Hall, Room 300) to process your transactions. Every day there will be sessions dedicated to specific HR and Finance transactions. Schedule coming soon.

  • Employee Self Service (ESS)

    ESS refers to the PeopleSoft pages where employees can view/update personal data, set up multiple direct deposit accounts, view paystub information, etc. Attend an ESS Open House Session to verify your data.
