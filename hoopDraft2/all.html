Accordion

Why are we moving to PeopleSoft?

Answer:
PeopleSoft will help us to develop the infrastructure to support our goal of becoming a Tier One research university. The Board of Regents has established that selected UT schools must move from the legacy DEFINE system to PeopleSoft. The Regents have provided $30 million to establish a shared services environment in which other schools will join us in our conversion from BIS/HRMS to PeopleSoft.

How will I be affected by this move?

Answer:
If you use BIS/HRMS or UT Direct, you will be affected by the migration to PeopleSoft. Those who use BIS/HRMS to do their jobs and to support their departments will be more impacted than those employees who simply use UT Direct. Training in the various modules will begin well before the scheduled go-live date.

Which other UT schools will be moving to PeopleSoft?

Answer:
UT Arlington, San Antonio, Permian Basin, Tyler, and System are all moving to PeopleSoft within our same timeline. UT Pan American and Brownsville are still pending a decision. UT Dallas has already implemented PeopleSoft.

Will our student information system (Banner/Goldmine) be converted to PeopleSoft?

Answer:
Not at this time. PeopleSoft will only be used for finance and HR business. The PeopleSoft module equivalent (Campus Solutions) was purchased, but at this time it will not be implemented. UT Dallas, Tyler, and Arlington have implemented the student information system within PeopleSoft.
