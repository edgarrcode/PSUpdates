Does the department create voluntary separation forms for all employees (including work-studies)?

Answer:
Yes.

Will the departments have access to view job data and position data?

Answer:
Yes; employees assigned the HR Reviewer role will have access to this information.

Do departments initiate the collection of UTEP property from an employee voluntarily separating from UTEP?

Answer:
Yes

How is property returned for voluntary separations?

Answer:
Either the individual turns in property and supporting documentation to corresponding areas (i.e., parking, key shop, library, IT, etc.), or the department management has to collect keys, codes, laptops and send to respective departments.

How do we handle voluntary separations from students with keys?

Answer:
Any UTEP property has to be acknowledged in the form.

How do we collect information to process new hires/rehires?

Answer:
The electronic IAP new hire/rehire form collects information needed for processing in PeopleSoft.

For early appointments, what is created first, position or funding?

Answer:
Positions. In the position process you are emailed the Position ID, which is required for creating funding.

How do I process a supplement?

Answer:
Faculty/instruction related duties are through the Provost (SAR form) and staff supplements go through HRAC.

How long before an HR transaction is processed after it has been submitted by the department?

Answer:
48 hours after all correct documentation and approvals have been submitted to HR.
