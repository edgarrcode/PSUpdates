Finance IAP Forms

Student Information Form

  • This form is required to set up a vendor record for non-employee students for the purpose of creating a travel authorization, or processing a payment. Submit a hard copy of this form to Accounts Payable.
  • Note: After the student has been entered into the system, submit an Employee Reimbursement, Vendor Payment, or Travel Authorization IAP form. See descriptions below to determine which IAP to process.
  • Download the Student Information Form

Travel Authorization

  • Authorize travel for an employee/student (when required) who will be traveling on university-related business for more than half a day, regardless of whether or not there is a cost to the University.
  • Note: One day after the traveler has returned from travel, the travel authorization initiator receives an email reminder to create a Travel Reimbursement. The initiator should create the Travel Reimbursement IAP form through the link in the email; the initiator does not need to create a separate Employee Reimbursement IAP form.
  • Access the Travel Authorization Form

Employee Reimbursement

  • Reimburse an employee who has incurred a university-related business expense, such as purchased food for the department, purchased supplies, etc.
  • Reimburse a student who has incurred a university-related business expense, such as mileage, registration fees, purchased supplies, etc.
  • Access the Employee Reimbursement Form

Vendor Payment

  • Process a non-PO payment for a vendor when a PO was not created.
  • Process a non-PO payment for a student who has provided a service such as note taking, APS services, etc.
  • Process a non-PO payment to process a refund for a student in a non-credit course.
  • Access the Vendor Payment Form

Budget Transfer Request

  • Request to transfer funds between projects or cost centers.
  • Access the Budget Transfer Request Form

HR IAP Forms

Position Request & Modifications

  • Request a new position due to departmental reorganizations, special projects, new grants or contracts, workload increases, position combinations, etc.
  • Request an update to a position due to reclassifications, inactivating positions, working title changes, position transfers, reorganizations, changes in full time equivalent (FTE) status, changes in Reports-To information, etc.
  • Access the Position Request & Modifications Form

New Hire/Rehire

  • Request to hire or extend the appointment for a student, faculty, or lecturer.
  • Access the New Hire/Rehire Form

Additional Pay Request

  • Request compensation not included in the budget for endowed chairs or endowed professorships.
  • Request a supplement not included in the budget for new department chairs, new program directors, A&P employees, or Classified employees.
  • Request compensation for teaching an overload or developing an online course during the Fall, Spring, or Summer semester.
  • Request compensation for administrative or research duties outside the normal scope of work, or for intra-university consultation.
  • Access the Additional Pay Request Form new

Position Funding Changes

  • Request a change to the funding source (cost center or project) of a position.
  • Access the Position Funding Changes Form

Access Request Form

Request Access to PeopleSoft

  • Request access to PeopleSoft to process finance transactions, or view department HR data.
  • Request Access to PeopleSoft Form
