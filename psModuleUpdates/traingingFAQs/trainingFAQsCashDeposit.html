What business unit do I use when entering a new deposit?

Answer:
Use “UTETR”. For Contracts & Grants, use “UTESP”.

Should I let the system automatically number a Deposit ID when adding a new deposit?

Answer:
No. Use a unique identifier for your deposits. Use the deposit number from the bank deposit. Doing so will make it easier to search for the deposit at a later date.
