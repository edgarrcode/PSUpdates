Our Partnership

Ricoh continues to expand its partnership with the university and its departments. We continue to look for opportunities which are beneficial to the departments and Ricoh.

We would love to have you come visit us at our service centers. Our open-door policy for departments remains in place.

The Ricoh Quality Commitment

Ricoh resolves to work in partnership with UTEP to provide a qualitative and quantitative solution that will better support your requirements.

We are committed to your total satisfaction.

We will measure our service levels through the use of surveys, as well as the constant solicitation of feedback by our Site Manager. Continual input from you will permit us to improve our operation to consistently meet your needs.
