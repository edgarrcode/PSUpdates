How To Order

Main Content

Use Miner Mall to order UTEP branded business cards, envelopes and letterhead.

All files needs to be sent as PDF format

Once you are logged in to Miner Mall follow these steps:

  1. Open the “Ricoh Print Products Order Form” by clicking the “Richo Print Shop Products” button.
  2. Complete your request form and submit it for approvals.
  3. Ricoh will send you an e-mail request to approve your proofs.
  4. Product, if proofs are approved by Thursday at 10 a.m., will be delivered next day.

Launch Miner Mall

Feel free to contact the Ricoh Print Shop or your departmental buyer if you have any questions on the use of this form.
