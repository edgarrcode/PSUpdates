Business Process Guidelines

Welcome to the Official Website for UTEP's Business Process Guidelines

Common business procedures have been documented and compiled into an online resource for the convenience of the campus community. The guidelines presented here are intended to be a supplement to the Handbook of Operating Procedures and departmental policies. In the event of any conflict between the Handbook of Operating Procedures and the Business Process Guidelines, the Handbook of Operating Procedures governs, as it is the official compilation of University policies.

As new procedures are documented they will be posted to this site. If you have a question about a posted procedure, please contact the VPBA Office. We hope you find this resource to be a useful tool in your daily operations.
