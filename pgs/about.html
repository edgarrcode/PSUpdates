Mission

The Purchasing & General Services department is committed to supporting the University's mission by offering services to the campus community with the highest legal, ethical standards encompassing professionalism, responsiveness and fiscal responsibility. UTEP also has a commitment to the HUB Program (historically underutilized businesses) providing equal access to business opportunities at the University for diverse suppliers. Purchasing policies and procedures will be adhered at all times.

Vision

Our vision is to be recognized as a leader in the educational industry in applying strategic supply management practices to achieve excellence in procurement producing a sustainable competitive advantage in every facet of our University's operation.

Scope of Responsibility

Core values of The Purchasing and General Services department are:

Integrity: Personal and professional integrity and high ethical standards in communication and behavior guide all that we do and are paramount in our performance.

Quality: We provide quality products and exceptional customer service at the best possible value to our customers by procurement practices that exceed customer requirements.

Commitment: We are committed to achieving the highest quality and commitment to continuous improvement in our daily activities.

Teamwork: We work cooperatively with shared purpose and vision, and promote collaboration. We value and respect the individual through recognizing everyone's skills, diverse range of experiences and contributions.
