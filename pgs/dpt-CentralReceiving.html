Mission

Central Receiving provides receiving and delivery services for all goods ordered by The University's teaching, research, administrative and project offices.

Scope Of Responsibility

Central Receiving ensures items ordered are received and accepted in accordance with the specifications and terms of the contract. They also ensure timely delivery to the department that requested the items, as well as providing receiving reports to Accounts Payable for payment to vendors.

Services

  • Receiving all material obtained by purchase order from vendors and transportation companies.
  • Performing receiving inspection to determine quantity delivered and inspect for damage.
  • Delivery to requesting departments and following acceptance period, forwarding Receiving Report to Accounts Payable for payment.

FAQs

Do you provide shipping services ?

Answer:
Yes, we provide parcel and freight shipping services for UTEP departments utilizing their appropiate UTEP account number. We do not provide U.S. Postal Service.

For U.S. Postal Services, please contact UTEP Mail Services at: (915) 747- 5677.

Can you tell me if my package was received ?

Answer:
Do you have a Purchase Order Number or Tracking Number? This will help locate your package.

Is this the Surplus Department ?

Answer:
No, please call Surplus Department at: (915) 747-7136

Do you have empty boxes ?

Answer:
We do not keep empty boxes. Please call the Warehouse Depatment at: (915) 747-8128

Can you deliver my package right now ? I need it.

Answer:
If a crew becomes available, they will be sent to deliver your package. You always have the option to pick up your package providng proper identification.

Where are you located ?

Answer:
We are located inside the Facility Services Complex, Building B.

Can you hold our equipment for a couple of weeks ?

Answer:
I'm sorry, but we only hold item's for 48 hours. The receiving department does not have space for storage.
