My Paycheck Profile

Main Content

You now have the ability to print your Earnings Statements and Form W-2 from the My Paycheck Profile web site designed by UT Austin. You may also change your address, your W-4 data and your direct deposit information directly from this site. Below, links are provided to UT Direct and general information about UT Direct. A link is also provided to the main page of MY PAYCHECK PROFILE, or you may use the specific links to access specific portions of My Paycheck Profile, such as My W-2 and My Earnings Statement among others.

Note that you must have a UT Employee Identification (UT EID) and password to access the secured web site.
