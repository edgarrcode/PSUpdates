APS Form

Main Content

The Authorization for Personal Services (APS) form is used as a basis for paying individuals providing consulting or specialized services to the University.

  • APS Form is required for payments of over $250.00
  • APS Form must be completed prior to routing for approval
  • Approvals should be obtained from the Director or Dean, and Vice President as well as Contracts & Grants and the Office of Research and Sponsored Projects (ORSP) if applicable (26 accounts)
  • Send completed original APS Forms to the Payroll office for approval
  • Payroll will return a copy of the APS form and a Memorandum with instructions for processing payment
  • APS form can be found on UTEP Forms Mine

    • Purpose of the APS Form

    The APS form is used to correctly classify an individual as an employee or independent contractor. This will allow Financial Services to accurately process IRS forms at year end.

    Information on the APS form includes the following:

    • Name, address, social security number (or UT EID), and citizenship status
    • Type and description of services to be provided
    • Employee status
    • Nepotism statement
    • Period of service
    • Amount of proposed payment and account information

    Employment Status

    If currently employed or has been employed with the University within the past year, the individual qualifies as an employee:

    • Ensure that I-9 and W-4 paperwork has been completed and filed with Human Resource Services.
    • Payments for these individuals will be processed through an OV7 document in BIS and submitted to the Payroll Office.
    • Departments without access to OV7 documents in BIS need to contact the Help Desk.

    Independent Contractor Status

    If not currently employed or has not been employed with the University within the past year, the individual qualifies as an independent contractor:

    • To determine if the independent contractor has a vendor ID, search in BIS using the GG3 or GG4 screens.
    • If the independent contractor does not have a vendor ID established with the University, a Payee Information Form (PIF) must be fully completed and sent to the Purchasing Office.
    • Payments to independent contractors will be processed through VP2 document in BIS and submitted to the Accounts Payable department.

    Please note: If the independent contractor is a non resident alien, send them to the Human Resource Services department to obtain access to Glacier for determination of tax treaty status.

    Helpful Hints

    • Make sure a social security number or UT EID is included.
    • Verify that the accounts being used to fund the APS form being generated have sufficient funds available.
    • Must verify that the account number being used for the transaction is correct and that the account that is being used is the proper account for payment of wages (use 20 sub account) or consultant fees (use 51 or 60 sub account).
    • Ensure that the Questionnaire on the APS form is filled out completely and accurately.
    • Document must be routed so that all pertinent authorizing signatures are included.
    • Ensure that if the individual is classified as an employee, the duties performed are outside of their normal scope of job duties.
    • For additional information pertaining to APS forms, please enroll in the APS Training Workshop in PACE or download the training materials at our home page under "Training Tools".
