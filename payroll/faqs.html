How do I get a copy of my W2?

Answer:

  1. For active employees, W2's are sent via campus mail by the end of January.
  2. For non-active employees, W2's are sent to the mailing address on file.
  3. Download a copy of your W2 from UT Direct (i.e.: must have High Assurance UT EID)
  4. Go to Payroll Services and request a copy (i.e.: must have identification)

When do I get paid?

Answer:

  1. Salaried (monthly) employees are paid on the 1st working day of the month.
    1. Monthly employees are paid for the previous month
  2. Hourly employees are paid semi-monthly on the 5th and 15th working day of the month.
    1. Hourly employees covering the 16th through the end of the previous month are paid on the 5th working day.
    2. Hourly employees covering the 1st through the 15th are paid on the 15th working day of the current month.

What is a Total Pay Card?

Answer:

  • It is a prepaid stored-value Visa debit card that is automatically loaded with your money each payday
  • It is accepted at participating ATM's, Visa merchants, and Visa member banks

What is OASI (FICA)?

Answer:
This stands for Old Age Survivor's Insurance. OASI is also known as Social Security. All employees are subject to withholding for OASI unless exempt.
