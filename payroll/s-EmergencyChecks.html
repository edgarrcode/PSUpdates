Emergency Checks

Main Content

Emergency checks may be requested for earnings that were due to be paid on a prior scheduled payroll run but were not processed due to incomplete records and/or appointments. Emergency checks are NOT regular payroll checks; however actual payment information is used to calculate the best estimate of net pay.

A regular payroll check for these earnings will generally be processed on the next scheduled payroll run, and the emergency payment received will be reflected on the earnings statement as an emergency pay deduction. If the amount of the emergency payment differs from the amount received through the regular payroll process, the difference will either be reflected as additional net pay or will be carried forward as a deduction on the next payroll check.

Who Can Authorize Emergency Checks?

Emergency checks must be requested by the authorized signer on the account. Checks may be requested for the following reasons:

  1. A monthly employee's appointment was not approved before the monthly payroll pull
  2. Departmental payroll voucher (manual voucher) is not received by payroll deadline
  3. Hourly employee fails to turn in a timesheet
  4. Department fails to approve the electronic payroll voucher

How are Emergency Checks Requested?

The authorized account signer can submit a memo or email to the following departments:

  1. Payroll Office (payroll@utep.edu) receives HOURLY employee requests
  2. Budget Office (budget@utep.edu) receives MONTHLY employee requests
  3. Memo or email should contain the following information:
    • Explanation of circumstances requiring an emergency check
    • Employee's name and UT EID
    • Pay period dates, gross pay amount, department account number
    • Copy of Hourly Payment Document (OV1) for hourly employee only

How are Emergency Checks Distributed?

Emergency checks are issued daily, and are available for pick up in Payroll Services, University Towers, 1900 Oregon Street, Suite 100, with a photo ID.

See following deadlines:

Email Received Emergency Check Available
Before 10:00 a.m. 3:00 p.m. same day
After 10:00 a.m. 3:00 p.m. next day

**NOTE: Circumstances do arise where this is not always possible. It is strongly suggested to call the Payroll Office to verify that a check is available for pick-up.

When Will Emergency Check Requests be Declined?

  • An Unapproved Monthly Appointment
  • An Unapproved Hourly Payment Document (OV1)
  • A Flat Rate Payment Document (OV2)
  • A Department Overtime Pay Document (OV5)
  • A Department Vacation/Float/Holiday Pay Document (OV6)
  • A Professional Services Payment Document (OV7)
