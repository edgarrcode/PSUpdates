Cancellation Policy

It is important to pay your insurance premiums on time to avoid cancellation. Payments can be made in full or in monthly installments. If the due date falls on a holiday or weekend, payment is due by the following business day.

Please note: Accounts that are two months past due may be subject to cancellation

Payment Options

The University currently offers three ways to pay: Automatic Payment, Mail, or Walk in

  1. Automatic Credit Card Payment
    1. You may give your credit card information to the Payroll department and authorize automatic deductions from the credit card (monthly, quarterly, yearly)
    2. Fill in your credit card number and expiration date
  2. Mail - Please send payments to:
    1. The University of Texas at El Paso, 500 W. University Avenue, University Towers, El Paso, TX 79968
    2. Check and money order payments
      1. Specify the amount of your payment
      2. Sign the payment coupon
  3. Walk in - We are always happy to see you
    1. Payroll Services: University Towers, 1900 Oregon Street, Suite 100
