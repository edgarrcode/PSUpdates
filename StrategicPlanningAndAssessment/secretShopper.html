Secret Shopper Survey

The alignment of the office of the Vice President for Business Affairs (VPBA) with the overall Mission and Vision of the University of Texas at El Paso is the main driver for the VPBA Dashboard.

Each shopper receives a survey via email and is asked to answer it right after visiting the departments they were assigned. They are also assigned tasks for online and phone shops to be completed at the VPBA Office in a controlled environment.

Currently the secret shoppers are sent out at the beginning of every semester to shop at the following departments:

  • Sodexho - Retail
  • Parking and Transportation
  • University Ticket Center
  • Student Business Services
  • University Bookstore
